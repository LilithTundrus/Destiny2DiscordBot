For additional help go [here](https://github.com/LilithTundrus/Destiny2DiscordBot)


Note: Don't Include brackets in the commands!


**%about**

Display information about the bot


**%nightfall**

Get the weekly Nightfall info


**%searchplayer <BattleTag#1234>**

Search for a player by their Battle Tag and see if they have a Destiny 2 profile


**%profile <BattleTag#1234>**

Get a destiny player's stats (PC only for now) by BattleTag (required)


**%item <item name>**

Get info about a weapon or set of armor (paging through multiple results not yet ready)


%xur

Get the wherabouts of the mysterious vendor Xur and what he has for sale (IN ALPHA!!)
